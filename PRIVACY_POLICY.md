# Privacy Policy — InstaAudio Downloader

**Last Updated:** March 31, 2026

## Overview

InstaAudio Downloader ("the Extension") is a browser extension that allows users to download voice messages from their own Instagram Direct Message conversations. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data.**

The Extension operates entirely within your browser. No data ever leaves your device or is sent to any external server.

## How the Extension Works

1. **Network Interception (Local Only):** The Extension monitors network requests made by Instagram in your browser tab to detect audio/voice message file URLs. This monitoring happens entirely locally in your browser.

2. **Audio Playback & Download:** When you choose to play or download a voice message, the audio data is fetched directly from Instagram's servers to your browser. No intermediary servers are involved.

3. **No Account Access:** The Extension does not access, read, or store your Instagram credentials, account information, messages, contacts, or any other personal data.

4. **No Analytics or Tracking:** The Extension does not include any analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, or tracking of any kind.

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Why It's Needed |
|---|---|
| `webRequest` | To detect audio file URLs in network traffic (locally, in your browser) |
| `downloads` | To save voice message files to your computer |
| `activeTab` | To identify which tab is running Instagram |
| `tabs` | To match captured audio to the correct browser tab |
| `host_permissions` (CDN domains) | To fetch audio files for playback from Instagram's content delivery servers |

## Data Storage

- Audio URLs are stored **temporarily in browser memory only** while the tab is open.
- When you close the tab or switch chats, stored URLs are cleared.
- **Nothing is written to disk** except files you explicitly choose to download.
- No cookies, local storage, or IndexedDB is used.

## Third-Party Services

The Extension does not communicate with any third-party services. All operations are performed locally between your browser and Instagram's servers.

## Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.
